About

The Picture House of Horrors is located in a historic theater building in Eau Claire, Wisconsin. As our name suggests, we exclusively show horror films, both new and old. We also provide horror-themed drinks and snacks.

The goal of the Picture House of Horrors is to create a space for horror-fanatics to come together and share their love for the genre. The theater ventures beyond the timeless spooky classics to show newer indie horror films as well, so younger and older generations can experience the variety that horror has to offer.

We are open Thursday through Sunday and have five theaters, each with 100 seats. Tickets must be purchased at the door.
